Attendance Portal!

This portal is for tracking attendance. The fundamental overview is that first, the student must register for a course using his rollno and face, and then his attendance for a class can be recorded his attendance using his face at the take attendance portal. His attendance will thereafter be shown in the check attendance site. We also have a gateway for assessing class strength that employs a very accurate face detection library to determine the number of faces in the classroom, which will assist teachers in keeping track of the number of pupils present.

Course Register

It is a student portal for Registering for the course using their face and Roll number. It contains 3 demo courses where students can register for any of the course and give attendanece for the same.

Class Participation

It's a teacher-only portal that lets you see how many people are in your class by looking at a single photo of the room. It calculates the number of pupils precisely and add the strength to the database using a highly accurate library that is more accurate than opencv.It is for tracking the students participation of a particular course and on a particular date.
